About Us

The HASSs AMV is one among the oldest and unique institution thriving in the vicinity of Hubli. The college is premises is very well connected with road, rail and air links. Since, last 57 years we are committed to provide quality health care to the society and unmatchable education to the young Ayurveda enthusiasts. The college is working and carrying forward the vision and mission of its founders and patrons. The college was committed to fulfill its promises in the past, and will be doing the same in future as well.
Laboratory: A well equipped laboratory is attached to the hospital, functioning all 7 days a week. We provide accurate investigations at a nominal and affordable cost. Apart from regular Laboratory investigations we have the facility for X ray and ECG.
Operation Theatre: A well equipped OT is functioning for the various surgeries.
Hostel: Separate hostel facilities are available for girls and boys. Under able in charge wardens and due security we give emphasis on decipline.
Mess: Functioning mess provides quality food at nominal and affordable rates.
Hospital facilities OPDs Various OPDs of different specialties gives there services to the attending patients under the expert hands of qualified doctors and specialists. IPD 120 bed hospital with general and special wards provides total care to the In patients. Handled by able staff including nurses and therapists, we give complete and holistic health care.
